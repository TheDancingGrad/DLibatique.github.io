Welcome to the online supplemental exercises and resources for Carl Ruck's Intensive Latin: First Year & Review. This site has been designed primarily to complement Boston University's Spring 2016 course, CAS CL 115, Intensive Latin. Here, you will find exercises designed to test your mastery of the grammatical concepts in the textbook and go further with them. There will also be videos aimed at developing a deeper understanding of Latin and English grammar and syntax. To find a specific chapter's exercises and videos or to visit the page for videos on general Latin and English grammar and syntax, please use the navigation links above.
